I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [Key Laboratory of Data Engineering and Knowledge Engineering](http://deke.ruc.edu.cn/) and the [School of Information](http://info.ruc.edu.cn/), [Renmin University of China](https://www.ruc.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. [Feng Zhang](https://fengzhangcs.github.io/).

My research interests lie in database systems, data compression, and GPU acceleration. I am particularly interested in building efficient compression and execution techniques for data-intensive systems.
